My vocation is a balance between design and technology. However, the circumstances have gradually taken me to other fields of knowledge during my job experience. Despite that, I always showed a genuine interest in designing digital experiences. Consequently, I decided to raise the pencil again and continue with the self portrait that I began 20 years ago.

Now that I have taken this decision, I have three expectations with the Interactive Media Design Program. The first one is to lose the fear of writing code; the second one is to recover hard design skills, and the last one is improve my technical English. I am sure I took the right path with Algonquin Collegue to achieve my purpose.
